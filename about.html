ABOUT US

MIMCA is the website which acts as a medium or platform for communication between deaf-and-dumb and sharpen people using sign languages.

The ultimate aim of our project is to provide a user friendly platform to establish clear and concise communication between deaf -and-dumb and sharpen people.

"Kindness is the best language which deaf can hear and dumb can speak"
